ZAKAT AND CHARITABLE FOUNDATION

  • Establish collective system of Zakat and help people understand its true spirit
  • Support honorable and decent livelihoods
  • Provide relief to victims of calamities and work towards their rehabilitation
  • Free distribution of meals to the needy
  • Spread the message of peace, harmony and justice
  • Conserve and protect the natural environment
  • Develop partnerships with individuals, communities and organizations to further the organizational goals

PASSION

We are passionate about the work we do and how it improves our communities.

DIGNITY

Protecting human dignity is at the heart of everything we do.

EXCELLENCE

We are a continuously learning organisation and always open to new ideas for improvement.

TRANSPARENCY

We are completely transparent in all our social and financial transactions.
