PROFILE OF THE TRUST
Zakat and Charitable Foundation Trust (ZCF), is a non-governmental, non-profit organization registered in Lucknow. It was founded by a group of scholars and professionals in 2022 with the goal of uplifting economically and socially disadvantaged communities.
MISSION
Zakat and Charitable Foundation aims to prevent and alleviate human suffering. We are an independent NGO, working for the destitute and underprivileged. We seek justice and transformation of people’s lives for the better. We serve the needy as a demonstration of Almighty Allah’s unconditional love, helping everyone regardless of gender, caste or religion.
VISION
Inspired by our faith, Zakat and Charitable Foundation is working towards tackling the root causes of poverty and striving to achieve equality for everyone. We believe that every human being has the right to good education, clean drinking water, food and livelihood so that they are able to support their family and community.
WHY ZAKAT & CHARITABLE FOUNDATION
We make a lot of effort to alleviate poverty at a global level, support education for children living in poor families and support their families as well as the community at large.
