Mobile Clinic

Goals & Objectives
Despite many efforts to improve access to quality health care, our city faces many challenges like increasing costs, negligible awareness about basic health regime and high rates of chronic disease. People who are most affected by it are the ones with low income, people who belong to rural communities.

  • Identify neighborhoods where residents have a lack of access to healthcare.
  • Provide disease prevention services with community partners.
  • Provide primary medical care counseling and services at no cost to all individuals regardless of their caste, sex, and religion.

Mission
The mission of the Mobile Clinic is to reduce health disparities by providing best practice, flexible, low-barrier primary care at no cost to individuals, who are unable to access the healthcare system.Â 
Vision
To create health program that creates a systematic approach equipping individuals and groups as advocates of health. Mobile health clinics will play an important role especially among vulnerable populations, in rural communities without easy access to healthcare facilities. For some people, mobile health clinics are the only means to lifesaving medical treatment and access to affordable health.
