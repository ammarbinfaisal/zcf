BRAINY & BRIGHT ACADEMY (BBA)
Brainy & Bright Academy (BBA) has been recently established, in Lucknow under the banner of Zakat & Charitable Foundation. The coaching will be provided for economically and socially deprived students, who have potential to crack NEET & IIT-JEE, in collaboration with GRAVITY CLASSES, LUCKNOW, as knowledge and academic partner.
Join the highest result generating coaching class in the town from city’s top tutors for guaranteed results and get upto 90% discount. Now NEET-IIT-JEE coaching is not difficult for under privileged students. Few concerned people of the town have joined hands with Gravity Classes Lucknow, a leading coaching center in the entire region.For needy and deserving students, 90% of the fee will be paid through scholarship.
Eligibility: Coaching will be provided to 12th pass students and repeaters (who have appeared in NEET-IIT-JEE).
Classes have commenced from 15th june
